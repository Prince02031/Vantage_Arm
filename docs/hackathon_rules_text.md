Introduction
The Hackathon segment of Techathon Nationals, the ﬂagship 
event organized by the IUT Robotics Society (IUTRS) is a 
national-level hackathon focused on Artiﬁcial Intelligence (AI) 
and IOT Integration. The event is designed to bring together 
innovative students to build impactful, scalable, and technically 
sound solutions within a time-constrained environment. 
The event features a two-stage structure:
● Preliminary Round (Online): Teams are given a problem 
statement based on which they will provide a realistic 
solution and,
● Final Round (Onsite): Selected teams will be given another 
problem set and required to develop a solution in an 
intensive live environment. 
Whether you’re a student looking to challenge yourself, a team 
seeking recognition, or an innovator ready to make an impact, 
Techathon offers the idea platform to transform your ideas into 
reality.
Event Date:  
Preliminary Round: 2nd July, 2026
Onsite Round: 10th July, 2026
Venue: Islamic University of Technology Campus , Gazipur

Frequently Asked Questions
Registration & Eligibility
Q: Can professionals participate?
A: No, Techathon is exclusively for students. You must be a 
full-time student at the time of registration.
Q: Can I change team members after registration?
A: No, team members cannot be changed after preliminary 
round registration. In exceptional cases (medical emergency, 
personal crisis), contact organizers for approval.
Q: Can I register as an individual?
A: No, the minimum team size is 3 members. Solo and 2-member 
teams are not allowed.
Q: Can team members be from different universities?
A: Yes, cross-university teams are encouraged and allowed.
Q: What if I'm waiting for exam results?
A: You're eligible if you've attended your ﬁnal exam.
Judging & Submission
Q: When will I get feedback on my preliminary submission?
A: Detailed feedback is not provided; only pass/fail notiﬁcation. 
However, you'll receive a score breakdown if you advance to 
ﬁnals.
Q: Can judges ask questions about my code?
A: Yes, judges may ask technical questions during ﬁnal round 
presentations. Be prepared to explain your implementation 
decisions.

Q: What if I don't ﬁnish my project?
A: You can still submit. Incomplete projects will be evaluated 
based on what's submitted.
Q: How are ties broken?
A: Ties are broken by Innovation score, then AI Integration score, 
then Technical Execution score.
Facilities & Logistics

throughout the event.

Q: What if there's no electricity or internet?
A: Organizers will provide backup generators and internet 
hotspots. However, teams should have mobile hotspots as 
backup.
